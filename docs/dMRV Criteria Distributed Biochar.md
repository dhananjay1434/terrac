## **dMRV Data Requirements — Distributed Biochar** 

Rainbow BiCRS Methodology  ·  Data points required from dMRV providers 

## **1. PROJECT-RELATED — MONITORED PER PROJECT OR ANNUALLY** 

## _Once, or once per year_ 

- Infrastructure & machinery data — kiln material type, weight, item lifetime (at project setup; updated when kilns change) 

- Kiln Operator training records 

- Kiln Supervisor site visit reports 

- Scale calibration proof 

## _Annually or when feedstock changes_ 

- Methane emissions measurement — carbon mass balance (open-kiln) or carbon mass balance / volumetric flow × concentration (closed-kiln); 3 representative kiln runs by independent accredited provider 

- Environmental pollutants including PAH (closed-kiln only) — one composite sample 

- Biomass-to-biochar conversion factor (if not directly weighing biomass) 

- Updated biomass leakage assessment 

- Dry bulk density per site (if volume-based measurement) 

## _Per verification_ 

- Quality oversight report from the Project Developer 

- Environmental pollutants — heavy metals + PAH (closed-kiln mandatory; open-kiln heavy metals only) _[V]_ 

## **2. OPERATION-RELATED — MONITORED REGULARLY FOR CONTINUOUS ISSUANCE** 

_Per kiln run_ 

- Type and amount of biomass input (direct weighing or yield conversion ratio) 

- Moisture content of biomass input (handheld moisture meter, ≥1 per 100 kg, min. 10 per run, photo uploaded to dMRV) 

- Amount of energy inputs for ignition — closed-kiln only (type + amount, including syngas combustion) 

- Temperature curve from real-time sensors proving pyrolysis occurred 

- Photographs of pyrolysis process — open-kiln only (flame curtain, quenching, flame height <0.5m) 

- Mass or volume of fresh biochar produced 

- Biochar sub-sample set aside for Site Composite Pile (date, time, location, kiln ID/QR, batch ID/QR, photos) _Per production batch_ 

- Volume or mass of biochar delivered to permanent end use 

- GPS coordinates of biochar spreading sites 

- Delivery tracking records — date, amount, production batch ID 

- Buyer/user name and contact details 

_Per transport event_ 

- Distance, weight, vehicle type, fuel consumed — for biomass and for biochar 

## **3. LAB-TEST-RELATED — FOR THIRD-PARTY VERIFICATION ONLY** 

_Per production batch (sent to accredited lab)_ 

- H/Corg ratio — elemental analysis _[V]_ 

- Organic carbon content — elemental analysis _[V]_ 

- Biochar moisture content — min. 3 samples (if measuring via mass after quenching) _[V]_ 

- Dry bulk density — if measuring via volume (Kiln Supervisor, calibrated equipment) _[V]_ 

- Inertinite content + residual organic carbon — 1000-year permanence pathway only (min. 500 Ro measurements) _[V] Annually or when feedstock changes (independent provider)_ 

- Methane emission rate — carbon mass balance or volumetric flow × concentration, calibrated instruments, signed report _[V]_ 

- Environmental sustainability and leakage compliance of biomass feedstock — once per verification _[V]_ 

[V]  Verification only — not required for routine monitoring reports.
